# EverJar Logo & Brand Style Guide

## 🎨 Logo Concept

### Primary Logo
**Visual**: A minimalist glass jar with a heart-shaped opening, containing floating note icons that transform into hearts as they rise.

**Typography**:
- **Primary Font**: "EverJar" in elegant serif script (like "Dancing Script" or custom)
- **Tagline**: "One note a day. A lifetime of love." in clean sans-serif (like "Inter" or "Poppins")

**Color Palette**:
- **Primary**: Warm Rose (#E8A87C) - represents love and warmth
- **Secondary**: Soft Cream (#F5F5DC) - represents memories and nostalgia
- **Accent**: Deep Teal (#2E8B57) - represents growth and longevity
- **Text**: Charcoal Gray (#36454F) - for readability

### Logo Variations
1. **Icon Only**: Just the jar with floating notes
2. **Wordmark**: "EverJar" in script font
3. **Full Logo**: Icon + wordmark + tagline

## 🌟 Brand Personality

**Voice**: Warm, intimate, nostalgic, encouraging
**Tone**: Gentle, loving, hopeful, celebratory
**Values**: Connection, memory, intentionality, love

## 📱 Brand Elements

### Patterns
- Subtle heart patterns in backgrounds
- Wavy lines representing notes flowing
- Soft gradients from cream to rose

### Icons
- 🫙 Jar: Primary symbol
- ❤️ Heart: Love and emotion
- 📝 Note: Daily ritual
- 🔒 Lock: Future memories
- 📅 Calendar: Time and anticipation

### Imagery Style
- Soft focus photography
- Warm lighting
- Couples in candid moments
- Close-up details (hands holding, notes being written)
- Pastel color grading

## 🎯 Usage Guidelines

### Logo Clear Space
- Minimum clear space: 1x logo height around all sides

### Color Variations
- **Full Color**: For digital and premium materials
- **Monochrome**: Charcoal on cream background
- **Reversed**: Cream on rose background

### Typography Hierarchy
- **H1**: EverJar script (48-72pt)
- **H2**: Clean sans-serif bold (32-48pt)
- **Body**: Clean sans-serif regular (16-24pt)
- **Caption**: Clean sans-serif light (12-16pt)

## 📱 Digital Assets

### App Icon
- Rounded square with jar icon
- Background: Gradient from cream to rose
- Icon: White jar with teal accent

### Social Media
- Profile Picture: Full logo on cream background
- Cover Photo: Pattern of floating notes
- Story Highlights: Custom icons for each section

## 🚀 Brand Applications

### App UI
- Primary buttons: Rose with white text
- Secondary: Teal with white text
- Backgrounds: Cream with subtle patterns
- Cards: White with soft shadows

### Marketing Materials
- Website: Warm, inviting design
- Social posts: Consistent color scheme
- Email: Branded templates with heart motifs

### Packaging (Future)
- Premium features: Branded boxes
- Memory books: Custom covers with logo

---

*This brand style creates an emotional, memorable identity that feels like a cherished keepsake rather than a cold app.*
