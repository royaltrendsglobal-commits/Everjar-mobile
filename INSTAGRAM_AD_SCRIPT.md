## 🎨 Visual Style
- **Colors**: Warm pastels (soft pink, cream, light blue)
- **Font**: Handwritten script for emotional feel
- **Icons**: Hearts, jars, locks, calendars
- **Mood**: Nostalgic, warm, intimate

## 📈 Targeting Strategy
- **Audience**: 25-35, relationships, self-care interests
- **Interests**: Love, relationships, journaling, mindfulness
- **Locations**: Major cities, relationship-heavy areas
- **Lookalike**: Users who engage with relationship content

## 🚀 Launch Plan
1. **Day 1**: Main carousel post + story series
2. **Day 2-3**: User-generated content reposts
3. **Day 4**: Behind-the-scenes story
4. **Day 5**: Limited-time offer (first 100 users get premium free)

## 💡 Viral Hooks
- **Emotional storytelling**: Focus on feelings, not features
- **Shareable**: "Tag your partner" prompts
- **FOMO**: Limited-time unlocks
- **Community**: Build around shared experiences

## 📊 Expected Results
- 15-25% engagement rate
- 5-10% click-through to download
- High share rate due to emotional content

---

*This script is designed to feel authentic and emotional, not salesy. The goal is to make people feel the magic of EverJar before they even download it.*
